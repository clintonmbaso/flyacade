G4SciT1L01 – Introduction to Taxonomy
Sunday, April 02, 2023

Objective:
- Students will understand the concept of taxonomy and its importance in organizing living organisms.

Activities:
1. Engagement (15 minutes): Start with a brief discussion on different types of living things students encounter daily. Ask questions to spark curiosity.
2. Introduction to Taxonomy (30 minutes): Explain the concept of taxonomy, emphasizing the need for classification in understanding the diversity of life. Use simple examples.

4. Group Discussion (15 minutes): Discuss the classifications students came up with, highlighting the importance of common criteria in grouping.
