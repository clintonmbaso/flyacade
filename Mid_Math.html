Middle Math Term 3
G1EngT2L01 – Using The
Sunday, April 02, 2023
Week 1: Introduction to Money
- Introduce coins (penny, nickel, dime, and quarter).
- Teach the names and values of each coin.
- Use play money for hands-on learning.

Lesson Evaluation
G1EngT2L03 – Using And
Sunday, April 02, 2023
Week 2: Counting Coins
- Continue working with coins.
- Practice counting various coin combinations.
- Play pretend store or "buying" games.

Lesson Evaluation
G1EngT2L04 – Or
Sunday, April 02, 2023
Week 3: Simple Addition (Up to 5)
- Introduce basic addition using objects and pictures.
- Start with addition problems within 5 (e.g., 2 + 3).
- Use visual aids like counting fingers and objects.

Lesson Evaluation
G1EngT1L01 – Words That Tell Where
Sunday, April 02, 2023
Week 4: Simple Subtraction (Up to 5)
- Introduce basic subtraction using objects and pictures.
- Begin with subtraction problems within 5 (e.g., 5 - 2).
- Use visual aids for subtracting objects.

Lesson Evaluation
G1EngT1L01 – Writing Month Names
Sunday, April 02, 2023
Week 5: More Money Concepts
- Continue exploring coins and their values.
- Discuss the idea of saving and spending.

Lesson Evaluation
G1EngT1L01 – Using Commas
Sunday, April 02, 2023
Week 6: Adding and Subtracting with Money
