2. Interactive Reading Exercise (20 mins): Provide a new engaging story. As students read, pause at intervals to ask comprehension questions. Encourage them to refer back to the text for answers.

B1EngT3L01 – Fluency and Expression in Reading
Sunday, October 22, 2023

Objective: Focus on developing fluency and expression while reading stories.

1. Opening Activity (15 mins): Discuss the importance of fluency and expression in storytelling. Demonstrate reading a passage with different expressions and ask students to identify the changes.

2. Interactive Reading Exercise (20 mins): Provide a short story and have students practice reading with fluency and expression. Encourage them to use appropriate tones and pauses.

3. Group Discussion (15 mins): Facilitate a discussion about the impact of fluency and expression on storytelling. Ask students to share their experiences and challenges.

4. Homework (10 mins): Assign a task where students record themselves reading a short passage with emphasis on fluency and expression.

B1EngT3L01 – Assessing Comprehension and Recap
Sunday, October 22, 2023

Objective: Assess comprehension through a more extended story and recap key concepts.

1. Review Session (15 mins): Begin with a quick review of comprehension skills, fluency, and expression. Discuss any challenges students faced in previous lessons.

2. Interactive Reading Exercise (20 mins): Provide a longer, more complex story. As students read, ask a mix of comprehension and analysis questions. Encourage them to express opinions about the story.

3. Group Discussion (15 mins): Facilitate a discussion on the longer story, focusing on deeper comprehension and critical thinking. Encourage students to share their interpretations.

4. Closing Recap (10 mins): Summarize key concepts learned throughout the lessons. Emphasize the connection between phonetic principles, comprehension, and storytelling. Allow time for questions and reflections from students.
