G3 SDS Term 1
G3SDST1L01 – Understanding Home Location
Sunday, April 02, 2023

Objective:
- Students will be able to identify and locate their home on a map.

Activities:
1. Begin with a discussion about the concept of maps and why they are useful.
2. Provide each student with a simple map of their neighborhood or create one collectively.
3. Have students mark the location of their home on the map using symbols or colors.
4. Discuss the importance of understanding home location for personal awareness and safety.
5. Homework: Ask students to bring a small, labeled drawing of their home to share with the class.

Notes:
- Emphasize the importance of recognizing landmarks or street names for orientation.
- Encourage students to ask parents or guardians for additional information about their home's location.
