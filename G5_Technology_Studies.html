G5 Technology Studies Term 1
G4SciT1L01 – Introduction to the Work Room
Sunday, April 02, 2023

Objective:
- Students will understand the importance of safety in the workroom.
- Students will acquire positive attitudes and values in designing and making artifacts.
- Students will learn the proper storage of tools.

Materials Needed:
- Visual aids (posters, images)
- Examples of tools and their uses
- Notebook or journal for each student

Lesson Duration: 1 hour

Introduction (10 minutes):
1. Begin by discussing the importance of safety in the workroom. Explain that the workroom is a space where students will be creating and building, but it's essential to prioritize safety at all times.
2. Show visual aids depicting potential hazards in the workroom and discuss them with the students.
3. Ask students to brainstorm why safety is important in the workroom. Write their responses on the board.

Main Activities (30 minutes):
1. Demonstration: Show students examples of tools commonly used in the workroom and explain their uses. Emphasize safety guidelines for each tool.
2. Group Activity: Divide students into small groups and give each group a scenario involving a safety hazard in the workroom (e.g., leaving sharp tools on the floor). Have them discuss how they would address the situation safely.
3. Role-playing: Act out scenarios where students demonstrate safe behaviors in the workroom, such as wearing safety goggles, using tools properly, and cleaning up spills promptly.

Conclusion (15 minutes):
1. Review the importance of safety in the workroom and key safety guidelines.
2. Have students write a reflection in their notebooks about what they learned today and why safety is essential in the workroom.

Class Notes for Learners:
- Safety in the workroom is crucial to prevent accidents and injuries.
- Always wear appropriate safety gear, such as goggles and gloves.
- Use tools properly and follow safety guidelines.
- Keep the workroom clean and organized to prevent hazards.

G4SciT1L06 – Introduction to First Aid and First Aid Box
Sunday, April 02, 2023

Objective:
- Students will understand the importance of having a first aid box.
- Students will learn the basic contents of a first aid box.
- Students will demonstrate how to use some basic first aid items.

Materials Needed:
- Visual aids (images of first aid boxes and their contents)
- First aid items (bandages, antiseptic wipes, adhesive tape, etc.)
- First aid box (real or simulated)
- Notebook or journal for each student

Lesson Duration: 1 hour

Introduction (10 minutes):
1. Start by discussing the importance of having a first aid box in various settings, such as at home, in school, or during outdoor activities. Emphasize that a first aid box can help provide immediate care for injuries.
2. Show visual aids of different types of first aid boxes and their contents. Discuss the purpose of each item.
3. Ask students if they have encountered situations where first aid was needed and how having a first aid box could have been helpful.

Main Activities (30 minutes):
1. Demonstration: Display a first aid box and its contents. Explain the use of each item, such as bandages for wrapping wounds, antiseptic wipes for cleaning cuts, and adhesive tape for securing bandages.
2. Role-playing: Divide students into pairs and assign each pair a common injury scenario (e.g., a minor cut or scrape). Have them practice using items from the first aid box to provide basic first aid to each other.
3. Group Discussion: Lead a discussion on when and how to use a first aid box effectively. Encourage students to ask questions and share their experiences with first aid.

Conclusion (15 minutes):
1. Review the key concepts discussed in the lesson, including the importance of having a first aid box and knowing how to use its contents.
2. Have students write a list of essential items they think should be in a first aid box in their notebooks.
3. Assign students to create posters or infographics illustrating the contents of a first aid box and how to use them.

Class Notes for Learners:
- A first aid box contains essential items for providing immediate care for injuries.
- Some basic items in a first aid box include bandages, antiseptic wipes, adhesive tape, and scissors.
- It's important to know how to use the items in a first aid box safely and effectively.
