B1EngT1L01 – Introduction to Logic
Sunday, April 02, 2023

Objective:
   - Understand the concept of logic as the study of reasoning.

Activities:
1. Discussion (15 minutes):
   - Begin with a simple question: "How do we know things or make decisions?"
   - Facilitate a class discussion to gather students' ideas.
   - Introduce the term "logic" as the study of reasoning and making conclusions based on information.

2. Definition (10 minutes):
   - Provide a kid-friendly definition of logic.
   - Use relatable examples, such as deciding what clothes to wear based on the weather or choosing a game based on preferences.

3. Interactive Game (15 minutes):
   - Engage students in a logic-based game, like a riddle or puzzle, to demonstrate how reasoning works.
   - Encourage students to explain their thought process when solving the game.

4. Group Activity (10 minutes):
   - Divide the class into small groups.
   - Each group comes up with a simple scenario where logic is applied, and they present it to the class.

Homework:
   - Ask students to think about one decision they made recently and share it in the next class, explaining the logic behind their choice.
