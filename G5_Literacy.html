B1EngT1L01 – Understanding Synonyms
Sunday, April 02, 2023

Objective:
Introduce students to the concept of synonyms and explain their significance in language development.

1. Introduction (15 minutes):
   - Define "synonyms" as words that have similar meanings.
   - Provide examples to illustrate the concept.
   - Discuss how using synonyms can add variety and depth to language.

2. Activity (20 minutes):
   - Engage students in a hands-on activity. Provide a list of words and have them find and write down synonyms for each.
   - Encourage discussion and sharing of findings to reinforce learning.

3. Application (15 minutes):
   - Have students create sentences using synonyms to demonstrate practical usage.
   - Discuss how synonyms can enhance communication and expression.

4. Review and Homework (10 minutes):
   - Recap the key points of the lesson.
   - Assign homework, such as finding and writing sentences with synonyms from their favorite books or articles.
