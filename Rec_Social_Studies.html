Reception SDS Term 1
R3SStT1L01 - Grandfather and grandmother
Wednesday, April 05, 2023

Methodology

 Introduce the lesson with a motivational song “ On family..........”
 Talk about members of the nuclear family
 Ask learners to talk about themselves.
 Ask learners to talk about the family members
 Display a chart showing an extended family
 Ask learners to identify the grandmother and grandfather on the chart.
 Ask learners to draw and colour the family member they have identified.

Activity

 Learners to identify the grandmother and grandfather.
 Learners to draw and colour pictures of the grandmother and grandfather.

Assessment

 Listen as learners identify their grandmother and grandfather.
 Observe as learners draw and colour pictures of the grandmother and grandfather.

R3SStT1L02 - Aunties, uncles and cousins (extended family)
Wednesday, April 05, 2023

Methodology

- Revise the lesson on grandfather and grandmother.
- Introduce the lesson with a song “we are together” we are a family.
- Ask learners what they know about the extended family.
- Ask learners to bring forward the pictures (Real) of their aunties, uncles, cousins (which
you had asked them to bring prior to the lesson.)
- Ask learners to identify the uncles, aunties and, cousins in the picture.
- Tell learners about the different relations e.g Auntie (sister of your mother or father)
cousin (daughter or son of your Uncle or Auntie).

Activity

- Learners to sing “we are together, we are a family”
- Learners to identify their uncles, aunties and cousins on the picture.
- Learners to mention if their aunties and uncles are brothers or sisters of their parents.

Assessment

Listen as the learners talk about their uncles, aunts and cousins.

R3SStT1L07 - Rooms and their uses
Wednesday, April 05, 2023

Methodology

- Ask learners to sing the song “somebody in the kitchen...”
- Introduce the lesson by revising the previous lesson on sizes of houses.
- Ask learners to identify and name the different rooms they have in their houses, e.g.
bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, sitting room and toilet.
- Ask learners to mention the use of each room in the house.
- Put learners in groups and let them choose group leaders.
- Let learners in groups discuss the use of the kitchen, bedroom, sitting room, bathroom,
toilet.
- Go round and listen to what the learners are discussing in their groups .
- Let each group present to the rest of the class.

Activity

- Learners to be in groups.
- Learners to choose a group leader.
- Learners to identify and name the rooms in their houses
- Learners to describe the use of each room in the house.
- Learners to sing the song “somebody in the kitchen...”

Assessment

- Listen to learners as they identify different rooms in the house.
- Listen to learners as they describe the use of the different rooms in their houses.

R3SStT1L08 - Advantages of obeying rules
Wednesday, April 05, 2023

Methodology

- Introduce the lesson with a game with instructions e.g. kambushi kalilalila
- Make sure the learners understand well the instructions so that they are not offended
when they are out of the game
- Ask the learners to mention why it is important to obey the rules both at home and
school. .

Activity

- Learners to mention some of the rules they know
- Learners to do the game, kambushi kalilalila
- Learners to mention why it is important to obey the rules

Assessment

- Observe if learners are able to follow instructions
- Observe if learners are able to obey the rules of the game.
- Listen if learners are able to mention some of the rules at home.
- Listen if learners are able to mention the importance of obeying rules at home and school.

R3SStT3L09 - Importance of Communication
Wednesday, April 05, 2023

Methodology

- Introduce the lesson with a poem in line with the lesson.
- Revise the previous lesson on a newspaper.
- Ask learners why people communicate to each other in the home – expected answers – to
give instructions, to let other family members know when we are not feeling well, to
know what other people want from us e.g. we communicate to our friends to ask for
pencils rubber or any items one would like to use.
- Mention that effective communication gives us the right information and allow people to
express their feelings. This promotes peace and understanding among family members
and friends.
- Ask learners to do a role play on what they should do when they are sick.
- Divide the learners in groups of fours to do a role play on the importance and
effectiveness of communication.

Activity

- Learners in groups of fours to do a role play on the importance and effectiveness of
communication.
- Learners to mention the importance of communication.

Assessment

Observe and listen to learners as they talk about the importance of communication.
