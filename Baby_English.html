B1EngT1L01 – Introduction
Sunday, April 02, 2023
Day 1: Introduction
- Start with a story or a short video that prominently features words with the "ah" sound.
- Teach the sound 'ah' by pronouncing words like "cat," "hat," "bat," and "mat" emphasizing the sound.
- Play a game where students listen for and raise their hands when they hear words with the "ah" sound in a short story or song.
