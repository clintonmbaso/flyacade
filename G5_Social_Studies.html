B1EngT1L01 – Introduction to Mapping and Locating Districts
Sunday, April 02, 2023

Objective:
Students will learn to locate districts on a map, focusing on Zambia, and understand the importance of maps in geography.

Materials:
- Map of Zambia
- Worksheets with district names
- Pens/pencils

Procedure:
1. Introduction (10 minutes):
   - Discuss the importance of maps and how they help us understand geographical locations.
   - Show a map of Zambia and point out its key features (borders, major cities, rivers, etc.).
   - Explain what a district is and its significance in administrative divisions.

2. Activity (15 minutes):
   - Provide worksheets with the names of districts in Zambia.
   - Instruct students to locate each district on the map and mark it with a pen/pencil.
   - Walk around the classroom to assist students as needed.

3. Discussion (10 minutes):
   - Review the completed worksheets as a class, discussing any challenges students faced.
   - Emphasize the importance of accuracy in map reading and locating places.

4. Homework (5 minutes):
   - Assign students to research and write a short paragraph about one district in Zambia, including its location, main features, and significance.

Class Notes:
- Definition of a district.
- Importance of maps in geography.
- How to locate districts on a map.
- Accuracy in map reading.
